Kellyn Croy Certified Dog Trainer

Love on four paws offer dog walking, boarding and training services. Certified by Dog trainer people- your dog will not only love their time spent with Kellyn but it will come home with better manners.
Leave your best friend with a best friend.


Kellyn Croy transferred from her former career as an LPN working in both care facilities and hospice centers to working with dogs two years ago and hasn't looked back. Now she spends her days playing with cute, cuddly dogs and couldn't be happier.
